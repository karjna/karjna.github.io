About

Karina is a smart and adaptive web developer, who first learned to code in 2014 by taking a computer science class at her university, Cal Poly San Luis Obispo. After discovering a passion in coding, she moved to New York City and attended Dev Bootcamp in 2015. After graduating, Karina moved back to her hometown Los Angeles where she found her job at Creativengine. She currently creates websites and web apps for Congress.
