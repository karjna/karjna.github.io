About

Karina is a smart and adaptive web developer, who first learned to code in 2014 by taking a computer science class at her university, Cal Poly San Luis Obispo. After discovering a passion in coding, she moved to New York City and attended Dev Bootcamp in 2015. After graduating, Karina moved back to her hometown Los Angeles where she found her first job at Creativengine, creating websites and web apps for Congress. She worked Refiinery29, a consumer media web platform, for two years. She is now employed at Better, a mortgage company.
